Services

Services

As expected of a prominent recruitment firm, our professional services are continuously expanding and adapting to meet the needs of our changing times. We always strive to deliver high-value, quality HR service and staff to every client for every open position. AS Infotech offers a wide array of services to meet every need of the client, working within your work culture with your timetable, budget, and needs in mind.

Our recruiters work in synchronization with you to classify and retain employees, culminating in lower turnover ratios and an enriched bottom line. Services include:

Permanent Staffing

Permanent Staffing refers to the process of hiring employees for long-term roles within a company. It ensures stability, skill continuity, and long-term growth for both employer and employee.

Payrolling Services

Payrolling services manage employee wages, tax deductions, compliance, and payroll processing efficiently, ensuring accuracy and reduced administrative burdens.

Contract Staffing

Contract staffing provides skilled professionals on a temporary basis, offering flexibility, cost efficiency, and quick hiring solutions.

Recruitment Services Outsourcing

Recruitment Services Outsourcing (RPO) streamlines hiring by delegating talent acquisition to expert providers, ensuring efficient and cost-effective hiring.

HR Outsourcing

HR outsourcing helps businesses delegate HR functions like recruitment, payroll, and compliance to external experts, improving efficiency and cost savings.

Payroll Outsourcing

Payroll outsourcing ensures accurate salary processing, tax compliance, and benefits administration, reducing risks and administrative burdens.

Gulf Hiring

Gulf hiring connects skilled professionals with job opportunities in the Middle East, including the UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, and Oman.
